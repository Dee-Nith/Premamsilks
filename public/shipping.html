Shipping Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Delivery Areas

We deliver across India (Pan-India shipping) and offer international shipping to select countries on request. Whether you are in a metro city or a smaller town, we ensure your saree reaches you safely and on time.

Shipping Charges

  • Free Shipping: On all orders above ₹25,000.
  • Standard Shipping: A flat fee of ₹500 is applicable on orders below ₹25,000.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 2-3 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Delivery Time

  • Within India: 5-7 business days from the date of dispatch.
  • Metro Cities: Delivery may be faster, typically 3-5 business days.
  • Remote Areas: May take an additional 2-3 business days.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an email with the tracking details. You can use the tracking number to monitor your shipment in real time. If you have any questions about your order status, feel free to reach out to us.

Packaging

Every saree from Premam Silks is packaged with the utmost care. We use premium gift-grade packaging that is silk-safe, ensuring your saree arrives in pristine condition. Each saree is wrapped in soft tissue paper, placed in a branded box, and sealed securely for transit.

International Shipping

International shipping is available on request. Please contact us via WhatsApp or email at premamsilks@gmail.com with your delivery address, and we will provide a shipping quote and estimated delivery time. International duties and taxes, if applicable, are the responsibility of the buyer.
