Shipping & Delivery

We take pride in delivering our precious creations with the utmost care

Domestic Shipping

We offer Free Shipping across India on all orders.

International Shipping

We ship worldwide. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the destination and the weight of the package. Please contact us via WhatsApp or email at premamsilks@gmail.com for international orders.

Processing Time

Ready-to-ship sarees are dispatched within 24 to 48 hours. Custom orders may take 7+ additional days.

Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email and WhatsApp. You can use this to monitor your shipment in real time.

Packaging

Every saree from Premam Silks is packaged with the utmost care. We use premium gift-grade packaging that is silk-safe, ensuring your saree arrives in pristine condition.

For any questions about shipping, feel free to contact us.
