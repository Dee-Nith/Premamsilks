Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At Premam Silks, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or make a purchase.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you place an order or create an account:

  • Name - To personalize your experience and process orders.
  • Email Address - For order confirmations, shipping updates, and communication.
  • Phone Number - For delivery coordination and order-related queries.
  • Shipping Address - To deliver your orders to the correct location.
  • Billing Information - Processed securely through our payment gateway.

How We Use Your Data

Your personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To fulfill and ship your orders accurately.
  • Communication: To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and respond to your inquiries.
  • Customer Support: To assist you with returns, exchanges, and any issues.
  • Improvement: To enhance our website, products, and overall shopping experience.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Payment Security

All payments on Premam Silks are processed securely through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We do not store your credit card, debit card, or bank account details on our servers. Your payment information is encrypted and handled entirely by Razorpay's secure infrastructure.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies on our website, primarily for:

  • Cart Functionality: To remember items in your shopping cart across sessions.
  • Authentication: To keep you logged in during your browsing session.
  • Preferences: To remember your preferences for a better experience.

We do not use cookies for advertising or third-party tracking.

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate our website:

  • Razorpay: For secure payment processing.
  • Cloudinary: For image hosting and optimization.
  • Firebase: For authentication, database, and hosting services.

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing data handling.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill your orders and provide customer support. Order records are kept for a reasonable period for accounting and legal compliance purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can ask us to correct any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data from our systems.
  • Opt-Out: You can unsubscribe from our communications at any time.
