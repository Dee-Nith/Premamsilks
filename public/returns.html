Returns & Exchange

Last Updated: February 2026

At Premam Silks, your satisfaction is our priority. If you are not completely happy with your purchase, we offer a hassle-free return and exchange process.

Return Window

You may initiate a return within 7 days from the date of delivery. Returns requested after this window will not be accepted.

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, the saree must meet the following conditions:

  • The saree must be unworn and unused.
  • All original tags must be attached and intact.
  • The saree must be in its original packaging.
  • The saree must not have been altered, washed, or damaged by the customer.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return or exchange:

  • Custom-made or altered sarees that have been tailored or customized to your specifications.
  • Accessories such as blouse pieces sold separately, jewelry, or other add-ons.
  • Sarees purchased during clearance or final sale events.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please contact us through one of the following methods:

  • WhatsApp: Message us at +91 7200123457 with your order number and reason for return.
  • Email: Send a return request to premamsilks@gmail.com with your order details and photos of the product.

Our team will review your request and provide you with return instructions within 24-48 hours.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned saree, your refund will be processed within 7-10 business days to your original payment method. You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been initiated.

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refund will appear in your account within 7-10 business days.
  • UPI / Net Banking: Refund will be credited within 5-7 business days.

Exchange

Exchanges are available subject to stock availability. If you wish to exchange a saree for a different color, design, or style, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. If the desired product is unavailable, we will process a full refund instead.

For any questions regarding returns or exchanges, please do not hesitate to contact us.
